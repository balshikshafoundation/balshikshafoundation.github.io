Get Involved.

At Bāl Shiksha Foundation, we believe that lasting change is created through people; individuals who care deeply, think thoughtfully, and act with compassion. Whether you are a donor, professional, student, or organisation, there are meaningful ways to be part of our journey and help children access education with dignity.

Partner With Us.

By partnering with us, you help remove barriers that prevent children from staying in school and create pathways for long-term opportunity.

How to Get in Touch.

If you’re interested in partnering with Bāl Shiksha Foundation, please write to us and mention one of the following in the email subject line, based on how you’d like to engage:

  • Individual philanthropists
  • CSR partnerships
  • Family foundations
  • Institutional funders

This helps us route your enquiry and respond more effectively.

Email: contact@balshikshafoundation.org

Volunteer With Us.

Our volunteering opportunities are designed for individuals who want to contribute their skills, time, and experience in thoughtful, purposeful ways. As an early-stage foundation, we value volunteers who are patient, collaborative, and aligned with our values.
We welcome volunteers and collaborators who are willing to engage with care, responsibility, and respect for the communities we serve. Opportunities may be short-term, project-based, or ongoing, depending on programme needs.

Fundraising & Resource Mobilisation.

Support our efforts to raise funds and build sustainable partnerships.You may contribute by:

  • Supporting fundraising campaigns and outreach
  • Assisting with donor communication and follow-ups
  • Helping organise small fundraising events or initiatives

Community Visits & On-Ground Support.

Participate in carefully planned community visits alongside the Bāl Shiksha Foundation team. You may:

  • Accompany during programme or school visits
  • Support documentation and observation
  • Assist with coordination during field activities

Teaching & Learning Support.

Contribute directly to children’s learning journeys through academic and co-curricular support. Depending on need, readiness and child-safeguarding guidelines, you may support:

  • Foundational learning in reading, writing, and numeracy
  • Academic support in subjects such as English or Mathematics

Mentorship & Child Engagement.

Support children beyond academics through encouragement, guidance, and exposure. You may contribute by:

  • Mentoring older students
  • Supporting life-skills or confidence-building sessions
  • Assisting in structured group activities
  • Creative or co-curricular activities that encourage confidence and expression
Important Note on Volunteering

Bāl Shiksha Foundation is committed to ethical engagement, child safety, and long-term impact. All volunteer roles are guided by clear processes, safeguarding principles, and programme readiness.

Interested in contributing?

We’d love to learn more about your interests and explore how you can be involved meaningfully. Drop us an email at contact@balshikshafoundation.org

Support Our Work

If you would like to support Bāl Shiksha Foundation directly, you can make a contribution in a way that’s most convenient for you. Your support helps us provide children with access to education, learning resources, and the stability they need to continue their education with dignity.
