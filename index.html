We are Bāl Shiksha Foundation

Every child deserves the chance to learn, grow, and step into a future filled with opportunity.
We see children not as isolated individuals, but as part of families, communities, and schools that shape their everyday lives.
A child’s wellbeing depends on the strength of these relationships. Our work places children at the centre, while engaging the people and systems around them, because families, communities, and schools create conditions where learning can truly take root.
