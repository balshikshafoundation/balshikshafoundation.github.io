Programmes

Bāl Shiksha Foundation is currently in its foundational phase. At this stage, we are focusing our efforts on a single, clearly defined programme that addresses one of the most immediate barriers to education. This focused approach allows us to work thoughtfully, responsibly, and with care, ensuring that every child we support receives meaningful and consistent assistance.

Current Programme: Learning without Barriers

Education Support Programme

For many families, the cost of education, even in government or low-fee schools can be overwhelming. Expenses such as school fees, uniforms, books, or transport often become the reason children discontinue their education. Through our Education Support Programme, we help bridge these gaps so children can remain enrolled and continue learning.


What It Includes.

Depending on individual need, support may include:

  • School fees.
  • Uniforms and shoes.
  • Books and stationery.
  • Transport to and from school.
  • Other basic educational requirements.

What This Enables.

By removing immediate financial and logistical barriers, Access Support helps:

  • Keep children enrolled in school
  • Improve attendance and continuity
  • Reduce stress on families
  • Create stability in a child’s educational journey

Who This Programme Supports

This programmes focuses on children who:

  • Are at risk of dropping out due to financial or situational barriers
  • Come from families facing economic or social vulnerability
  • Have a strong desire to continue their education but lack resources

Looking Ahead

As Bāl Shiksha Foundation grows, we plan to gradually expand our work beyond individual support, responding to needs we observe on the ground. Future areas of support may include:

  • Supporting school amenities and facilities, where limited infrastructure affects learning conditions
  • Providing learning kits or educational supplies to groups of students, classrooms, or communities
  • Group-based educational support, where multiple children face similar barriers
  • Collaborative initiatives with schools or local organisations, aimed at improving access and continuity

These initiatives will be introduced carefully and responsibly, guided by:

  • On-ground learning and community feedback
  • Availability of resources and partnerships
  • A commitment to child safety, dignity, and sustainability

Our focus will remain on practical, needs-based support that strengthens educational access and learning environments.

How You Can Support The Programme(s)

The programme(s) could be made possible through the generosity of individuals and partners who believe in education as a pathway to dignity and opportunity.
