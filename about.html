Bāl Shiksha Foundation

For many children, education is out of reach and we feel strongly it should be a basic right and not a privilege. Millions of children in India face barriers such as poverty, migration, disability, or lack of support that prevent them from accessing education. We work towards removing these barriers with compassion and responsibility. We, as an organisation, are committed to equitable access to education for vulnerable children.

Our Purpose.

Empowering underprivileged children in India, supporting them through their entire educational journey—from early schooling through to higher education. Inspired by a decade of personal commitment and having witnessed the transformative impact of sustained support, our foundation is dedicated to expanding opportunities for more children. We believe that every child deserves access to high-quality education and, through long-term financial assistance, we aim to help them realise their full potential and shape a brighter future for themselves and their communities.

Our Mission.

All children should have access to a high quality education. To support children at risk of being left behind by enabling access to education and holistic support. To provide financial support to as many children as possible in their educational journey. More children in education will lead to more opportunities for them in their lives and careers, local communities will benefit and those who support / donate will have contributed to a powerful cause.

For it is in giving, that we receive.

What We Believe.

Origin

The origins of Bāl Shiksha Foundation lie in a simple act; supporting a few children individually and watching how education rebuilt their confidence, stability, and futures. These experiences shaped our belief that small, timely support can profoundly change a child’s life. Over time, we encountered families who desperately wanted to educate their children but lacked resources. Many children were bright, curious, and eager, yet prevented from learning due to circumstances beyond their control. The Foundation was created to build a structured, responsible way to reach more children like them.

The need we intend to serve

Children affected by poverty, instability, migration, family loss, disability or lack of support are often unable to stay in school. We want to ensure these children are not left behind. We believe that every child deserves the dignity of opportunity, Education is one of the most powerful tools for long-term change, and together, i.e. donors, communities, and supporters, we can open doors that would otherwise remain closed.
