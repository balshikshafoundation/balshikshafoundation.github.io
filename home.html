Welcome to Bāl Shiksha Foundation

Every child deserves the chance to learn, grow, and step into a future filled with opportunity.
Bāl Shiksha Foundation works to ensure that children from disadvantaged backgrounds are not left behind.

Learn more.

Our Mission.

Millions of children in India face barriers such as poverty, migration, disability, or lack of support that prevent them from accessing education.

We at, Bāl Shiksha Foundation, believe that timely, compassionate support can change the course of a child’s life.

What we Do.

For many families, the cost of education, even in government or low-fee schools can be overwhelming. We help bridge these gaps so children can remain enrolled and continue learning. Depending on individual need, support may include:

  • School Fees
  • Uniforms and Shoes
  • Books and Stationery
  • Transport to and from school
  • ther essential educational requirements

We believe in impact that is:

  • Sustainable: Support leads to long-term change.
  • Transparent: Donors see clear outcomes.
